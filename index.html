Hi! I'm a post-doc at Stanford, advised by Shreyas Vasanawala and John Pauly. My research interests are in signal processing, medical imaging, and inverse problems.

I did my undergrad (2013) and PhD (2018) at UC Berkeley, where I had the pleasure to work with Miki Lustig for 7 years. I worked on sparse signal representations, fast reconstruction algorithms, and open-source computational packages.

In my Berkeley days, I also collaborated with Kannan Ramchandran on elegant sparse FFT algorithms. And in 2017, I did a wonderful summer internship with Peyman Milanfar to connect kernel methods with proximal operators.

Extreme MRI: Large-scale 3D Dynamic MRI from Continuous Acquisitions
with Xucheng Zhu, Joseph Cheng, Kevin Johnson, Peder Larson, Shreyas Vasanawala, and Miki Lustig

~100GB 3D dynamic MRI can be reconstructed using a compressed low rank representation and a stochastic algorithm.

Accelerating Non-Cartesian MRI Reconstruction using k-space Preconditioning
with Martin Uecker, and Miki Lustig

Non-Cartesian MRI reconstructions can be efficiently accelerated by preconditioning the convex dual problem.

Approximating Proximal Operators with Kernel Methods
with Peyman Milanfar, and Pascal Getreuer

Any kernel based method can be approximated with a proximal operator with an appropriate regularization function. And vice versa.

General Phase Regularized MRI Reconstruction using Phase Cycling
with Joseph Cheng, and Miki Lustig

Robustness against phase wraps in iterative reconstruction can be achieved by randomly shifting the wraps in space over iterations.

Multiscale Low Rank Matrix Decomposition
with Miki Lustig

Decomposing a matrix into block-wise low rank matrices of different scales can be done with a convex program and works well for many imaging tasks.

2D Sparse Fast Fourier Transform using Sparse Graph Codes
with Samir Pawar, and Kannan Ramchandran

2D FFTs can be performed in O(k log k) time for k-sparse spectrums by cleverly subsampling the signal.

Robust 4D Flow Denoising using Divergence-Free Wavelet Transform
with Martin Uecker, Umar Tariq, Albert Hsiao, Marc Alley, Shreyas Vasanawala, and Miki Lustig

4D flow data can be denoised robustly by thresholding the non-divergence-free wavelet coefficients.
